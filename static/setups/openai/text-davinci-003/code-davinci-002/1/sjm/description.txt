The flag of Svalbard and Jan Mayen has a width-to-height ratio of 1:2. The flag is divided diagonally from the lower hoist to the fly. Both the upper triangle and the lower triangle are white. The upper triangle has an off-centered Norwegian red-white-blue cross on it and the lower triangle has two gold colors occupying two-thirds of the triangle, and one arrow pointing towards the hoist, occupying the remaining one-third of the triangle.
